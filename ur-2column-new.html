Summer Stroll & Social

Please join your fellow UC Santa Cruz graduates in Seattle for a leisurely afternoon walk around Green Lake, followed by a wine and cheese reception at the nearby home of a UCSC alum. Reminisce about walks through the redwoods, reinvigorate your Banana Slug pride, and build a network for the future.

Saturday, August 18, 2012
3:00-4:30 p.m. guided or self-paced walk
around the lake, as you choose
4:30-6:00 p.m. wine and cheese reception
at the nearby home of a UCSC alum*

Register online by August 15. You'll have the chance to meet some of the nearly 2,000 UCSC alumni who reside in the northwest region. Families are welcome. There are playgrounds and a skatepark at Green Lake, just blocks from the reception location.

*The location of the reception will be provided via email after you register for the event. There is ample on-street public parking along East Green Lake Way N, between N 51st and N 55th St.

Questions about this event? Contact Laura Caldwell at laura.caldwell@gmail.com or (831) 425-1027.
